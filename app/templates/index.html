Rules

The rules of Rock! Paper! Scissors! are as follows.

Each player can choose any of the 3 objects. The players choices are then compared. The results of the following logic determines the winner:

  • Rock! defeats Scissors! by blunting their blades (it's not the right type of stone to sharpen them)
  • Scissors! defeats Paper! by cutting it up! (fair enough!)
  • Paper! defeats Rock! by wrapping it up (it's a win of sorts I guess 🤷‍♂️)
  • Both players choosing the same object creates a vortex that... nah, it just results in a draw. Press Home and play again 'til you get a winner?
